New Contract won for Bloor Homes in Coventry.

20th February 2020

We are delighted to announce we have secured the contract for Bloor Homes in Coventry.

This is the first contract award for Bloors and we are looking forward to getting started on site and working alongside Bloors on this development.

The job consists of 81 Plots, 2922m2 of Roads and Sewers and the contract duration will be approximately 2 years.

We look forward to setting up and delivering this contract safely and to the highest possible standards and efficiencies.

New Contract won for Persimmon Homes at Rothwell in Northampton.

10th February 2020

We are delighted to secure the contract for Persimmon Homes in Rothwell in Northampton.

This contract award further cements our great relationship with Persimmon Homes.

This contract is 420 plots over 5 years with Roads and Sewers for the entire job along with the 278 Works going into the job.

We look forward to starting on this amazing contract and thank Persimmon for choosing Paul John for delivering.
